Simple Fajita

A simple fajita recipe that does not require much time nor effort to make nor consume. 10 minutes to make, 10 minutes to cook and 10 minutes to eat. Easy!

  • 400g sliced chicken filet
  • 1 pack tortillas
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 jar banana peppers
  • 1 tub of sour creme
  • quacemole (honestly just make as much as you could possibly can or your wallet afford)
  1. Fry up the meat
  2. chop the bell pepper
  3. chuck it all on the tortilla
  4. Remember you can never have enough banana peppers or guac
