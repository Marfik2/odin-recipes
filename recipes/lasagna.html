Simple Lasagna

A simple lasagna recipe that does not require much time nor effort to make or consume. 10 minuts to make, 10 minuts to cook, 10 minuts to eat, Easy!

  • 1 pack of lasagna plates
  • 500 ml of tomato sauce of your choice
  • 400 g of minced meat
  • A block of cheese of your choice
  • sprinkle of oregano
  • garnish for the fancy people
  1. Fry up the mince on the pan
  2. Add the sauce and bring to a boil
  3. Start off by adding one layer of the sauce in a deep dish
  4. Layer with plates and sauce
  5. Proceed to put the final layer with all the cheese in the world
  6. Finish off with a sprinkle of oregano
  7. Throw the garnish in the bin, who needs it 'emma'right?

That's it, enjoy! Go live you life! Life is too short to make food!
