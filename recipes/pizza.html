Simple Pizza

A simple pizza recipe that does not require much time nor effort to make nor consume. 10 minutes to make, 15 minutes (unfortunately) to cook, 10 minuts to eat. Easy!

  • Store bought base
  • Store bought sauce
  • Store bought cheese
  • Store bought pepperoni
  1. Put the sauce on the base
  2. Put the pepperoni on the sauce
  3. Put the cheese on the pepperoni
  4. Put the pizza in the oven
  5. Put the pizza in your mouth
